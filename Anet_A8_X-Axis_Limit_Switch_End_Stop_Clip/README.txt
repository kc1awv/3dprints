# Summary

The recent Anet A8 models have been shipped with injection moulded Z-axis nut supports and the screws supplied to secure the X-axis limit switch to it are the wrong size to fix it securely. So i designed this clip to do the job. 

This is only a minor upgrade to the Anet A8 and may not even be necessary, but it gives me piece of mind!
